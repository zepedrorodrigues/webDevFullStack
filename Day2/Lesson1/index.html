Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm mlik
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the fliling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the mlik with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add it to the mixture.
  3. Add the flour and salt and mix untli it forms a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Place the dough in a bowl and cover with a damp cloth. Leave to rise for 1 hour.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the fliling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the fliling over the dough.
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  10. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min untli lightly brown.
